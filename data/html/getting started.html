==getting started==

when you start a new game you only have $2000 so you will need to make the simplest money making machine posible
to start go to any sell point
place a belt facing into that sell point
place a smelter pointing into that belt
place a belt pointing into the back of the smelter
place a spawner pointing into the seccond belt
click on the spawner and set it to iron ore
you will now slowly make money

==tips==

when crafting tings you want to be as effishent as posible so you want to make shure all the ratios are correct btewwn craftings
ex: when making steel you need exactly 1 coal spawner and 1 iron spawner
a list of all the information required to determinbe these ratios is located on the materials page
